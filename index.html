The Core Diagnosis

This funnel sells the vehicle, not the destination.

Root Cause Identified

The team is leading with the mechanism (intuition, altered states, brainwave science) instead of the dream outcome (wealth, better decisions, career performance).

Nobody wakes up and says "Hey, I need to have better intuition." There's always a goal behind it. Intuition is just a mechanism — but what is the goal? Why would someone want higher intuition? Because they want to make money. Because they want to make the right decisions every time. Because they want to identify the right strategy and direction for their business.

The dream outcome for this funnel should be: "How to make the right decisions all the time so you can build wealth faster." The mechanism is the Silva Ultramind Method. The proof is the science — but right now, the team is using proof AS the outcome, which is fundamentally wrong.

Until this inversion is fixed at every stage — ads, landing page, emails, masterclass, sales page — no amount of creative testing or budget increase will move the needle.

Step 0 — The Framework

Before fixing anything: the team must understand this

This framework applies to every pathway, every outcome, every funnel going forward. It's the DNA of every direct-response funnel that converts.

Outcome → Mechanism → Proof

Every element of the funnel must clearly distinguish these three layers. The funnel leads with the Outcome, introduces the Mechanism as the surprising solution, and uses Proof to make the mechanism credible. Proof backs up the mechanism — it explains WHY this solution works. Case studies prove the outcome is achievable.

🎯
Outcome
What the prospect's life looks like AFTER. Always emotional, always tangible. This is what they actually want.
For Silva / Wealth:Make million-dollar decisions with confidence • Build wealth through clarity • Make the right bet every time • Never second-guess a major choice
⚙️
Mechanism
HOW they get there. The method, system, technique. This is the bridge between their pain and the transformation.
For Silva:The Silva Ultramind Method — the technique used by high performers for 60 years, backed by neuroscience
🔬
Proof
WHY they should believe it works. Science proves the mechanism. Case studies prove the outcome is achievable for someone like them.

The Testing Error — This Is What The Team Gets Wrong

The team is testing mechanisms against each other and using proof as the outcome.

"Science" is NOT an outcome — it's a proof layer that makes every funnel more credible. "Intuition" is NOT an outcome — it's a mechanism. "Creativity" is NOT an outcome — it's abstract and means nothing to a buyer. The only angle close to an outcome is "Career Advancement" — and it has the best D30 ROAS at 185.3%. This proves the point exactly.

Correct Testing Matrix

Test OUTCOMES against each other, not mechanisms. Then layer mechanisms and proof INTO each:

Outcome 1: Wealth / Business Growth — "How top entrepreneurs use mental training to make million-dollar decisions"
→ Mechanism: Intuition training via Silva Ultramind
→ Proof: Science (60 yrs research) + Vishen's $100M story + entrepreneur case studies

Outcome 2: Healing / Health — "The clinically studied technique that doubled cancer survival rates"
→ Mechanism: Alpha/Theta brain states via Silva Ultramind
→ Proof: Science (Dr. Simonton cancer study) + Vishen's skin healing + health case studies

Science should be included in EVERYTHING. It backs up the mechanism. It's the credibility layer that overcomes the #1 cold-traffic objection: "is this woo-woo?" But it's never the headline — the OUTCOME is always the headline.

Critical: The Show-Up Emails Must Prove the Value Equation

By the time prospects reach the masterclass, they should already be warmed up on every element of the equation.

The show-up emails need to prove it's easy to do (anyone can meditate, 15 min/day), prove anyone can achieve this (diverse case studies — not just athletes), prove the perceived likelihood is high (science + real results), and make the dream outcome feel urgent and real. Can we bring them to the app and platform and give them warm-up material so they have something to do in the next 3 days to get excited? Can we give them pre-material that primes them for the class? YES — and that's the fix.

Key Patterns in the Data

◆ Pattern: Situation-Based Ads Outperform Mechanism-Based Ads
  • Top-row ads leading with SITUATIONS ("high stakes," "big decisions," "pressure") get 2.58–2.90% CTR — people resonate with the real-world scenario
  • Bottom-row ads leading with INTUITION AS A CONCEPT ("Intuition Isn't a Gift," "Intuition Isn't Random") get 0.95–1.03% CTR and $65+ CPL
  • The video ad got 7.92% CTR (!) — people click out of curiosity — but 0% ROAS. High CTR ≠ high intent
  • But even the better-angled ads produce 0% downstream revenue because the landing page doesn't continue the story the ad started
◆ Why Branded Search Is the Only Winner
  • Google Search on "Silva" has 132.5% D1 ROAS and 166.5% D7 — it's printing money
  • These people are SEARCHING for Silva Method — they have pre-existing intent and brand awareness
  • The funnel doesn't need to do heavy lifting for them — they already know what they want
  • This is not scalable. The audience is capped at people who already know about Silva
  • Real growth must come from cold Facebook — and that's where it's falling apart
  • Branded search converting doesn't mean the funnel is good. It means the audience is good. The funnel's job is to take someone who has NEVER heard of Silva and walk them from pain → realization → method → register
❌ What's Wrong
  • Ads lead with mechanism: "The Silva Projection Technique for Intuition," "Intuition Isn't a Gift, It's a Skill," "Intuition Isn't Random, It's Trainable"
  • "1.2M Students. Intuition Unlocked." — pure social proof + mechanism label. No emotional hook. "Intuition Unlocked" means nothing. Unlocked for what? To do what?
  • Even situation-based ads ("How High Performers Stay Calm When Stakes Are High") produce 0% ROAS because the landing page doesn't continue the story — someone clicks expecting clarity under pressure and gets a page about training intuition
  • The scent trail from ad to landing page is completely broken
  • Testing mechanisms vs. mechanisms (science vs. intuition vs. creativity) instead of outcomes vs. outcomes
✓ How to Fix
  • Lead every ad with the outcome + mechanism clearly stated: "How Vishen Built a $100M Company by Training His Intuition Using the Silva Ultramind Method" — not "trusting his hunch" (what is a hunch? no mechanism), but clearly: trained his intuition + Silva Ultramind Method
  • Target the entrepreneur/high-performer avatar explicitly — use THEIR language: decisions, bets, strategy, scaling, pressure, stakes
  • Use science as a credibility subline, not a headline: "Backed by 60 years of neuroscience research" under an outcome-driven headline
  • Ensure ad-to-page scent match: if ad says "stay calm when stakes are high," the LP headline MUST continue that exact thread. Consider dynamic headlines per ad group
  • Test OUTCOMES (Wealth vs. Healing), not mechanisms. Layer science into everything as credibility

Current Page Copy — Full Audit (10 Problems)

❌ Problem 1: The page doesn't know who it's talking to
  • There's no avatar definition driving the copy. Who is the person landing here? A burned-out entrepreneur? A woman in her 40s disconnected after years of people-pleasing? A high-performer who operates on logic? The page talks to everyone and therefore talks to no one. Every great LP makes the reader feel "this was written for me." This one doesn't.
❌ Problem 2: Headline opens a loop it never closes — PAS violated
  • "You've Ignored Your Gut. Then Watched It Be Right." → "Train Your Intuition to Be Razor-Sharp and Accurate." That's Problem → Solution with ZERO Agitation in between. Where's the section that says "and here's what that costs you — the job you didn't take, the relationship you stayed in too long, the opportunity you let pass because you couldn't trust yourself"? The page skips the entire pain-agitation phase.
❌ Problem 3: Subheadline is proof before desire
  • "The protocol Vishen used to double his close rate. The same technique Tony Gonzalez and Bianca Andrescu use to win." This is credibility and social proof — but it's too early. The prospect hasn't been sold on WHY they need this yet. You're name-dropping before establishing relevance. Proof amplifies desire — it cannot create it.
❌ Problem 4: "What You'll Learn" is a syllabus, not a promise
  • Bullet 1: "Why Intuition Is a Trainable Skill — Not a Rare Gift" → mechanism
  • Bullet 2: "The Brainwave State Where Intuition Awakens" → mechanism/science
  • Bullet 3: "How Vishen Used Intuition to Transform His Career Overnight" → Vishen's story, not the prospect's future
  • Bullet 4: "The 3 Scenes Technique: Your First Step Into Intuitive Living" → mechanism
  • 3 of 4 bullets are about the METHOD. A cold prospect reads "The Brainwave State Where Intuition Awakens" and thinks "this sounds like a science lecture" or "this is woo-woo."
❌ Problem 5: Vishen's story positioned as biography, not the prospect's future
  • The section about Vishen doubling his sales is compelling — but it's framed as HIS story, not YOURS. "You'll learn the exact technique Vishen used when he was broke, failing at sales, and desperate" would connect it to the prospect's transformation. The bridge from his story to their future is completely missing.
❌ Problem 6: Learning journey has zero emotional pull
  • "Understand How Intuition Actually Works" → "Activate Your Intuition" → "Start Noticing the Signals." This reads like a course syllabus. Compare to: "Walk away knowing exactly why you've been stuck" → "Experience a live shift in decision-making clarity" → "Leave with a practice that starts working within 48 hours." Same content, completely different energy.
❌ Problem 7: FAQs address wrong objections
  • FAQs cover "Do I need meditation experience?" and "Is this free?" Those aren't the real objections. Cold traffic objections for intuition: "Is this scientifically backed or pseudoscience?" "Will this work for an analytical, logic-driven person?" "I've never been intuitive — can this change?" "How is this different from just meditating?" None addressed.
❌ Problem 8: Social proof is volume, not transformation
  • "15,000+ participants" and "1.2 million students" and "rated 4.94" — popular, but not proof that it WORKS. Where's "I used this to finally trust my gut on a deal and saved $200K" or "I stopped overthinking every decision"? People don't buy because something is popular — they buy because someone like them got a result they want.
❌ Problem 9: No urgency or stakes
  • "Playing for a limited time only" is the only urgency. No cost-of-inaction framing. What happens if you DON'T develop this? You keep making decisions from fear. You keep ignoring signals. The page never makes the prospect feel the cost of staying where they are.
❌ Problem 10: Fatal scent break from ads
  • Someone clicks "How High Performers Stay Calm When Stakes Are High" and lands on a page about training intuition, brainwave states, and the Silva Method. The ad promised clarity under pressure. The page delivers a masterclass registration about intuition. That disconnect alone kills the 3.42% conversion rate.
